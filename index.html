H K T

We love music

HKT is a Vietnamese pop group formed by Hai Au Entertainment in 2005 with 3 members: Ho Gia Hung, Ly Tuan Kiet and Pham Linh Phuong (stage name Ti Ti). The first three letters of these names are combined to form the official name of the group.From its inception to its disbandment, HKT was one of the most controversial music groups in the Vietnamese music industry in the early 21st century. With a strange and bizarre fashion style and music that was considered a "disaster" and was looked down upon and criticized by many people in Vietnam, this music group still achieved much success and attention from the Chinese music market. Music experts say that most of HKT's career was built on scandals, but one thing that cannot be denied is that HKT's music is very catchy and easy to listen to.
